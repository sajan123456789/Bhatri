Psychology Career Guide India 2026: Skills, Salary & Jobs

Published: March 2026

India's **Psychology market** is witnessing a massive transition. As mental health awareness enters the mainstream, the demand for licensed professionals across clinical and corporate sectors is skyrocketing.

🧠 Career Paths in Psychology

•

Clinical Psychologist: Focuses on diagnosing and treating complex mental disorders.

•

I/O Psychologist: Applies behavioral science to optimize workplace performance.

•

Counseling Psychologist: Assists individuals with life transitions and everyday stress.

💰 2026 Salary Structure (Experience Wise)
