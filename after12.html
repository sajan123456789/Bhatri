Career Options After Class 12

Choose the right degree, career path and competitive exams after 12th based on your stream and interest.

After 12th Science

Best for students interested in technology, medicine and innovation.

Popular Degrees:

  • BTech / BE
  • MBBS / BDS
  • BSc (Science / IT / Data)

Career Paths:

  • Engineer
  • Doctor
  • Data Analyst
  • Research Scientist

After 12th Commerce

Ideal for business, finance and corporate careers.

Popular Degrees:

  • BCom / BCom (Hons)
  • BBA
  • Integrated MBA

Career Paths:

  • CA / CS
  • Banking & Finance
  • Management
  • Entrepreneurship

After 12th Arts

Suitable for creative, analytical and society-focused students.

Popular Degrees:

  • BA (Hons.)
  • BA LLB
  • BJMC / Mass Communication

Career Paths:

  • Lawyer
  • Civil Services
  • Journalist
  • Teacher / Professor
