What Is Freelancing?

Freelancing means working independently. You offer your skills to clients online or offline and get paid per project or per hour.

You are not tied to one company. You choose your clients. You decide your working hours.

Freelancers work in many fields like design, writing, coding, marketing, and video editing.

Why Freelancing Is Growing in 2026

  • Remote work is common
  • Companies hire global talent
  • Internet access is everywhere
  • People want flexible careers
  • Skills are valued more than degrees
