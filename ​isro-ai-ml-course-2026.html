1. What is the ISRO IIRS AI/ML Course?

The Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) actively aims to build technical capacity in geospatial technologies through its distance learning programs. These 2026 online modules dive deeply into integrating Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning with geodata analysis and earth observation.

2. 2026 Course Schedule & Details

According to the latest 2026 IIRS Distance Learning Programme calendar, there are two major AI/ML specialized slots this year:

Course Name Duration & Tentative Dates Registration Start Date
Geodata Processing using Python and Machine Learning Feb 09, 2026 - Feb 26, 2026 (2 Weeks) January 19, 2026
AI/ML for Geodata Analysis August 03, 2026 - August 14, 2026 (1 Week) July 13, 2026

3. Who is Eligible to Apply?

  • Students & Researchers: Highly beneficial for active university students and academic researchers.
  • Academic Background: You must be a Graduate in Engineering or a Post Graduate in Science (Computer Science, Mathematics, Physics, Geoinformatics, etc.).
  • Diploma Holders: Those with a Diploma in Engineering plus at least three years of relevant working experience are also eligible.
  • Prerequisite: A basic working knowledge of computer programming and geospatial data is expected.

4. How to Fill the Registration Form (Step-by-Step)

Do not pay any individual or organization; the course is officially fully free. Follow these steps to register:

  1. Access the Portal: Go to the official ISRO EDUSAT website: https://elearning.iirs.gov.in/edusatregistration/.
  2. Select the Course: Pick the relevant AI/ML course when the registration window opens (e.g., Jan 19 or Jul 13).
  3. Submit Personal & Educational Info: Fill out your basic identity data and highest qualification accurately.
  4. Professional Details: Upload required validation documents (like ID proof or university nomination).
  5. Final Submission: Submit the application and await final approval status via email.

5. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. Is the ISRO AI/ML course completely free?

Yes, IIRS-ISRO online courses are offered completely free of cost for students. No fee is charged.

2. What are the upcoming course dates in 2026?

The "Geodata Processing using Python and Machine Learning" course will run from Feb 09 - 26, 2026, and "AI/ML for Geodata Analysis" is scheduled for Aug 03 - 14, 2026.

3. Who is eligible?

Graduates in Engineering, Post-Graduates in Science disciplines, or Engineering Diploma holders with 3 years of experience are eligible to apply.

4. Do I get a certificate?

Yes, upon completing the program and maintaining the required class attendance, candidates receive an official free certification.

5. Where can I apply?

You must apply online via the official EDUSAT registration portal: https://elearning.iirs.gov.in/edusatregistration/.
