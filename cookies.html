Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how CareerSteps uses them, and how you can control your cookie preferences.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently, improve user experience and provide information to website owners.

How CareerSteps Uses Cookies

CareerSteps uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • To understand how users interact with our website
  • To analyze website traffic and performance
  • To improve content, layout and user experience
  • To support advertising and analytics services

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly and cannot be disabled in our systems.

Analytics Cookies: These cookies help us understand how visitors use the website so we can improve performance and content quality.

Advertising Cookies: Advertising partners such as Google AdSense may use cookies to show ads that are relevant to users based on their interests.

Google AdSense Cookies

CareerSteps may use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google uses cookies such as the DoubleClick cookie to serve ads to users based on their visits to this and other websites.

Users can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies may be placed by third-party services that appear on our website. CareerSteps does not control these cookies and recommends users review the privacy and cookie policies of third-party providers.

How to Control Cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of certain parts of the website.

Most web browsers allow users to manage cookie preferences through settings or options menus.

Your Consent

By continuing to use the CareerSteps website, you consent to the use of cookies in accordance with this Cookie Policy.

Updates to This Cookie Policy

CareerSteps may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and users are encouraged to review it periodically.
