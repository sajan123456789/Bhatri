1. Introduction: The High-Stakes Choice

In 2025, students face a hyper-competitive market where Artificial Intelligence (AI) is reshaping every industry. CareerSteps has analyzed thousands of data points to create this guide to help you navigate from Class 10 to a high-paying career.

2. After Class 10: Choosing Your Foundation

The choice of stream after 10th is the first major step. Whether you choose Science, Commerce, or Arts, you are setting a foundation for the next 40 years of your life.

The Science Stream (PCM/PCB)

Science remains the most flexible stream. PCM is ideal for Engineering, Architecture, and Data Science. PCB is the gateway to Medicine, Biotech, and Genetics.

The Commerce Stream

For those who love strategy and finance, Commerce offers paths like Chartered Accountancy (CA), Investment Banking, and Fintech.

The Arts & Humanities Stream

Arts is no longer just "social studies." In 2025, it leads to high-value roles in Corporate Law, UI/UX Design, Psychology, and Management.
