Frequently Asked Questions

Which stream is best after Class 10?
There is no single best stream. The right choice depends on a student’s interest, aptitude, strengths, and long-term career goals rather than marks alone.

Is diploma better than degree?
Diploma courses are skill-based and job-oriented, while degree programs provide deeper academic knowledge and long-term growth opportunities.

How does CareerSteps help students?
CareerSteps provides structured career guidance, educational resources, and AI-based assessment tools to help students make informed career decisions.
