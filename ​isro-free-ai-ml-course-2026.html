1. What is the ISRO IIRS AI/ML Course?

The Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) actively builds technical capacity in geospatial technologies through its EDUSAT distance learning programs. These 2026 online modules dive deeply into integrating Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) with satellite geodata analysis and earth observation.

Course Name Duration & Tentative Dates Registration Start Date
Geodata Processing using Python & Machine Learning Feb 09, 2026 - Feb 26, 2026 (2 Weeks) January 19, 2026
AI/ML for Geodata Analysis August 03, 2026 - August 14, 2026 (1 Week) July 13, 2026

2. What Will You Learn? (Core Syllabus)

This is not a generic AI course. It is specifically tailored for spatial data and earth observation. The curriculum typically includes:

🐍 Python & ML Basics

  • Introduction to Python programming for data science.
  • Supervised vs. Unsupervised Machine Learning concepts.
  • Using libraries like NumPy, Pandas, and Scikit-Learn.

🌍 Spatial AI Applications

  • Deep Learning (CNNs) for processing Satellite Imagery.
  • Feature extraction and land cover classification.
  • Real-world case studies on remote sensing data.

3. Eligibility & Career Benefits

While the course is open to a wide audience, specific academic backgrounds are preferred for official university coordination:

  • Academic Background: Graduates in Engineering (B.E/B.Tech) or Post Graduates in Science (Computer Science, Mathematics, Physics, Geoinformatics, etc.).
  • Diploma Holders: Those with a Diploma in Engineering plus at least three years of relevant working experience.
  • Career Impact: Adding a certification directly from ISRO/IIRS significantly boosts a data science or software engineering resume, showing practical application of AI in complex datasets.

4. Certification Criteria & Exam Rules

To maintain the prestige of the certificate, ISRO has strict guidelines for successful completion:

  • Attendance Requirement: Candidates must maintain a minimum of 70% attendance during the live online sessions.
  • Online Assessment: An objective-type online exam will be conducted at the end of the course.
  • Award: Only those who meet the attendance criteria and pass the assessment will receive the official digital certificate.

5. How to Fill the Registration Form (Step-by-Step)

Do not pay any individual or organization; the course is officially fully free. Based on the official portal, follow these exact steps to register your account:

  1. Access the Portal: Go to the official ISRO EDUSAT website: elearning.iirs.gov.in/edusatregistration.
  2. Enter Basic Details: Fill in your Full Name and enter your active Email Address twice (for confirmation).
  3. Create a Strong Password: Set a secure password. It must contain at least 8 characters, including 1 uppercase letter, 1 lowercase letter, 1 number (0-9), and 1 special character (!@#$%^&*).
  4. Verify Captcha: Enter the alphanumeric code displayed in the captcha image accurately.
  5. Click Register: Hit the blue "Register" button to create your account. After this, you can log in to complete your profile and select your specific AI/ML course.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. Is the ISRO AI/ML course completely free?

Yes, IIRS-ISRO online courses are offered completely free of cost. No registration, tuition, or examination fee is charged.

2. What are the upcoming course dates in 2026?

The "Geodata Processing using Python and Machine Learning" course runs from Feb 09 - 26, 2026. "AI/ML for Geodata Analysis" is scheduled for Aug 03 - 14, 2026.

3. How do I get the official ISRO certificate?

You must maintain at least 70% attendance during the live sessions and pass the final online objective examination.

4. Can college students apply?

Absolutely. Active university students, especially those in engineering (B.Tech) or science streams (BSc/MSc), are highly encouraged to apply.

5. Where can I apply?

You must apply online via the official EDUSAT registration portal: elearning.iirs.gov.in/edusatregistration/.
