01. The Death of "Coding or Nothing"

Remote work 2026 mein koi "Perk" nahi balki standard ban chuka hai. AI ne basic programming ko itna fast bana diya hai ki companies ko ab programmers se zyada aise logon ki zarurat hai jo complex problems ko simplify kar sakein.

Aaj ke digital market mein aapka degree se zyada aapka Proof of Work matter karta hai. Agar aap ek creative mind hain jo logic ko samajhta hai, toh aap un coders se zyada earn kar sakte hain jo sirf manual tasks karte hain.

03. 4-Week Transition Roadmap

Zero se start kar rahe ho? Yeh structured path follow karo:

Week 1: Find Your Niche

Apne interest ko identify karein. Kya aap Ops (Operations) mein ache hain ya Design mein? Use the CareerSteps Assessment to find your fit.

Week 2: Create Ghost Projects

Bina clients ke portfolio kaise banayein? Dummy projects banayein. Ek real business problem uthayein aur uska solution document karein.

Week 3: Personal Branding

LinkedIn is your new resume. Apne projects ko 'Results' ke form mein dikhayein, 'Responsibilities' ke form mein nahi.

Week 4: Cold Outreach

Remote-first startups ko target karein. $2000/month remote roles ke liye global outreach zaroori hai.
