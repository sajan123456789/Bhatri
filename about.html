About CareerSteps

Empowering 10th & 12th students with AI-driven career intelligence.

Who We Are

CareerSteps is a student-focused career guidance platform designed to help school students make informed and confident career choices after Class 10 and Class 12. We believe that every student has a unique potential that needs the right direction at the right time.

Our platform combines advanced data analysis with simple psychological insights to ensure that no student feels left behind in this fast-paced competitive world.

Our Mission

To eliminate career-related stress and confusion by providing transparent, unbiased, and easily accessible guidance to every Indian student for free.

Our Vision

To become the first point of contact for students whenever they think about their future, bridging the gap between education and industry.

Why CareerSteps Was Created

Many students choose streams or careers due to peer pressure, family expectations or lack of proper information. This often leads to confusion, dissatisfaction and regret later in life.

CareerSteps was created to be that "Digital Mentor" that guides you based on YOUR strengths, not someone else's opinion.

What We Do

  • ✅ Stream Selection after 10th
  • ✅ Degree & Job paths after 12th
  • ✅ Interest-based Career Assessments
  • ✅ 900+ Careers Database access

Our Future Goal

We are constantly updating our database to include "New-Age Careers" like AI Ethics, Space Tourism, and Green Energy Management. We aim to guide 1 million students by the end of 2026.
