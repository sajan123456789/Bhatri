About CareerSteps

Empowering students after 10th & 12th with AI-driven career clarity — not guesswork.

Who We Are

CareerSteps is a student-first career guidance platform built for Indian students who feel confused while choosing streams, degrees, or career paths after Class 10 and Class 12.

We believe career decisions should be based on aptitude, interests, and real opportunities — not pressure, trends, or random advice.

Our Mission

To remove career confusion by providing free, unbiased, AI-powered guidance that helps every student discover their true potential with confidence.

Our Vision

To become India’s most trusted digital career mentor — guiding students from school to a meaningful and future-ready career.

Why CareerSteps Was Created

Most students choose careers based on marks, peer pressure, or family expectations. This often leads to dissatisfaction, course changes, or regret later in life.

CareerSteps was created to act as a digital mentor that guides you based on your strengths — not someone else’s opinion.

What We Do

✅ Stream selection after Class 10
✅ Degree & career paths after Class 12
✅ Interest-based AI career assessments
✅ 900+ verified career options

Our Future Goal

We continuously update our platform with emerging careers such as Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Space Technology, Sustainability, and more.

Our goal is to guide over 1 million students by 2026 with clarity, confidence, and purpose.
