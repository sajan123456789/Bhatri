What is Bachelor of Computer Applications?

BCA is a 3-year undergraduate degree that focuses on computer applications and software development. Unlike a B.Tech which covers both hardware and software, BCA is more focused on the application side of things.

In 2026, as AI and Cloud Computing dominate the market, the BCA syllabus has evolved. Students now learn Python, Java, Machine Learning, and Web 3.0 technologies.

Entrance Exams You Must Know

To get into a top-tier college, you usually need to clear an entrance exam. These test your Mathematics and Logic.

  • IPU
    IPU CET Highly popular for Delhi/NCR colleges.
  • CUET
    CUET (UG) The standard for Central Universities.
