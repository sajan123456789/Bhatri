Why Choose Arts After 10th?

Arts stream is ideal for students interested in Humanities, Social Sciences, Law, Psychology, Media, Design, Government Services and Public Policy. Students can pursue careers in UPSC, Law, Journalism, Psychology, International Relations, Teaching and many emerging creative industries.

FAQs

Is Arts a good stream after 10th?

Yes. Arts offers careers in Law, UPSC, Psychology, Journalism, Design and many other fields.

Can Arts students become IAS?

Yes. Arts students are among the largest group of successful UPSC candidates.

Can Arts students become lawyers?

Yes. Arts students can pursue BA LLB through CLAT and other law entrance exams.

Is Arts easier than Science?

Arts requires analytical thinking, communication skills and subject understanding.

What are the highest-paying Arts careers?

IAS Officer, Lawyer, Psychologist and International Relations Expert are among the top-paying Arts careers.
