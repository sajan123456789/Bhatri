The truth is simple. Good career planning reduces mistakes. It saves time. It saves money. And most importantly, it gives clarity.

What is Career Planning?

Career planning means thinking about your future job and preparing for it step by step.

  • Understanding your interests
  • Knowing your strengths
  • Researching careers
  • Learning required skills
  • Setting long-term goals

Career planning is not about choosing the highest salary. It is about choosing the right direction.

Step 1: Know Yourself

Before choosing any career, ask yourself:

  • What subjects do I enjoy?
  • Do I like practical work or theory?
  • Do I prefer stable job or business?
  • Am I comfortable with competition?

Honest answers will help you more than any advice.
