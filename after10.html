Career Options After Class 10

Confused after 10th? Understand streams, subjects, exams and career paths clearly.

Science Stream

Best for students interested in technology, medicine and research.

Subjects:

  • Physics
  • Chemistry
  • Maths / Biology

Future Careers:

  • Engineering
  • Medical
  • Data Science
  • Research

Commerce Stream

Ideal for business, finance and management oriented students.

Subjects:

  • Accountancy
  • Business Studies
  • Economics

Future Careers:

  • CA / CS
  • Banking
  • Finance
  • Entrepreneurship

Arts / Humanities

Suitable for creative, social and analytical thinkers.

Subjects:

  • History
  • Political Science
  • Sociology

Future Careers:

  • Law
  • Civil Services
  • Teaching
  • Journalism

How to Choose the Right Stream?

  • ✔ Choose based on interest, not peer pressure
  • ✔ Understand long-term career options
  • ✔ Consider your strengths & learning style
  • ✔ Avoid choosing only based on marks
