Career Options After Class 10

Confused after 10th? Understand streams, subjects, exams and career paths clearly.

Science Stream (PCM/PCB)

Science is the most preferred stream for students aiming for technical and medical fields. It offers a deep understanding of the natural world and technology. With the integration of AI in 2026, Science students have massive opportunities in emerging sectors.

Core Subjects:

  • Physics: Dynamics, Optics, and Thermodynamics.
  • Chemistry: Organic, Inorganic, and Physical Chemistry.
  • Mathematics: Calculus, Algebra, and Trigonometry.
  • Biology: Zoology, Botany, and Human Anatomy.

Top Careers (2026-2030):

  • Software & AI Engineering
  • MBBS, BDS, and specialized Surgery
  • Data Science & Machine Learning
  • Biotechnology & Genetic Research
  • Aeronautical & Space Sciences

Commerce Stream

Commerce is the backbone of the global economy. It is ideal for students with strong analytical and mathematical skills who want to enter the world of business, finance, and trade.

Core Subjects:

  • Accountancy: Financial statements and taxation.
  • Business Studies: Management and trade ethics.
  • Economics: Macro and Micro economic analysis.
  • Mathematics: Statistical data and finance math.

Top Careers (2026-2030):

  • Chartered Accountancy (CA)
  • Investment Banking & Wealth Management
  • Financial Risk Analysis
  • Corporate Law & Secretarial Practice (CS)
  • E-commerce Business Management

Arts & Humanities

Arts is no longer just about history. It is a highly creative and analytical stream that prepares students for civil services, legal professions, and modern media roles.

Core Subjects:

  • Psychology: Study of human behavior and mind.
  • History & Political Science: Governance and culture.
  • Geography & Sociology: Social structures and environment.
  • Design: Graphics, Fashion, and UX/UI design.

Top Careers (2026-2030):

  • Civil Services (IAS/IPS Officer)
  • Corporate & Criminal Law
  • Product Design & User Experience
  • Mass Communication & Journalism
  • Psychology & Mental Health Therapy
