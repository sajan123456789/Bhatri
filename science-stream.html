Science Stream After 10th

Complete guide to PCM, PCB, PCMB, careers and salary.

🔬 What is Science Stream?

Science stream is one of the most popular choices after Class 10. It is ideal for students interested in Technology, Engineering, Medicine, Research, Innovation and Future Careers. Science opens pathways to careers in Artificial Intelligence, Software Development, Healthcare, Space Research, Biotechnology, Cyber Security and many other high-demand industries.

Is Science difficult after 10th?

Science requires consistency and practice, especially in Maths and Science subjects.

Which is better PCM or PCB?

PCM is ideal for Engineering and Technology, while PCB is suitable for Medical and Life Sciences.

Can PCM students become IAS?

Yes. Students from any stream can prepare for UPSC Civil Services.

Can PCB students enter AI?

Yes. With additional coding and data skills, PCB students can enter AI and Data Science fields.

Is Science the best stream after 10th?

The best stream depends on your interests, strengths and long-term career goals.
